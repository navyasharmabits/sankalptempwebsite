SANKALP

Public Administration · General Affairs · History · Law

Sankalp is a student-led public administration, general affairs and history club committed to fostering informed discussion, analytical thinking and responsible campus engagement. With structured Wings, regular debate-oriented General Wing meets, research-based activities and written reports, Sankalp provides a transparent and participatory platform for students to contribute ideas, analyse contemporary issues and develop administrative and organisational skills. Through consistent member interaction and collaborative initiatives, it aims to build a knowledgeable, disciplined and cohesive community

Who we are

Established as the primary student body for policy and civic engagement, Sankalp currently brings together 60+ active members across multiple wings. Our work includes weekly debate meets, policy research, administrative reports, archival projects and campus coordination. We are committed to transparency, accountability and improving student representation.
