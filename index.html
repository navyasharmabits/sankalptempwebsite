SANKALP

Public Administration · General Affairs · History · Law

Sankalp is a student-led public administration, general affairs and history club committed to fostering informed discussion, analytical thinking and responsible campus engagement. With structured Wings, regular debate-oriented General Wing meets, research-based activities and written reports, Sankalp provides a transparent and participatory platform for students to contribute ideas, analyse contemporary issues and develop administrative and organisational skills. Through consistent member interaction and collaborative initiatives, it aims to build a knowledgeable, disciplined and cohesive community

Who we are

Sankalp unites 60+ active members across its wings as the campus hub for policy and civic engagement. From weekly debate meets and structured policy research to administrative reporting, archival work and campus coordination, we fuel informed discussion and organised collaboration. We strive to promote transparency, accountability and a healthy, participatory campus environment

Implementation Wing

The soon-to-be-launched Implementation Wing will serve as Sankalp’s centre for civil services preparation. It will organise organised study hours, conceptual workshops, peer discussions and strategy sessions tailored to foundational subjects. The wing is designed to promote disciplined preparation, strengthen understanding of core topics and encourage collaborative academic growth among aspirants

Events & Operations

Democracy within Sankalp

Sankalp operates on a democratic framework where key office-bearers are chosen through member elections. This ensures that leadership reflects the collective mandate of the organisation. The role of the Leader of Opposition is built into the structure to encourage constructive scrutiny, open discussion and healthy internal debate with significant systems and committes to balanc power
