Welcome to Sankalp

The Public Administration, General Affairs & History Society

Sankalp is the official platform for structured policy research, administrative coordination, responsible student governance and academic discourse. Our mission is to strengthen civic awareness, internal governance and structured dialogue on campus through organized activity, wings, deliberations and reports.

What We Do

Sankalp functions through structured administrative channels. Our members participate in weekly meetings, inter-wing coordination sessions, research compilations and report submissions. We have conducted debates, policy reviews, drafting sessions and collaborative administrative work with over 60 active members and growing.

We specialize in developing internal governance frameworks, debate structures, research policies, student body representation and official documentation. The society follows a constitutionally mandated structure with checks, procedures and roles.
